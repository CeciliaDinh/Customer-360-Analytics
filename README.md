# Customer 360 Analytics  
### RFM, Churn & Growth Insights using PySpark  
**Author:** Dinh Thi Thanh Hang  

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project builds a **scalable customer analytics system** using **Python & PySpark** to analyze transactional retail data and uncover:

- Customer behavior patterns  
- Churn drivers  
- Product preferences  
- Geographic growth opportunities  

The analysis is structured using the **3P-G Framework**:

> **Product – People – Place – Growth**

👉 Goal: **Transform raw data into actionable business strategies**

---

## 🧩 Problem Statement

Despite strong revenue, the business faces several key challenges:

- ⚠️ High **customer churn (~32%)**
- 🇬🇧 Heavy reliance on **United Kingdom market**
- 🎯 Weak **customer retention strategy**
- 📉 Revenue driven by **seasonality (holiday peaks)**
- 🧾 Data quality issues (missing / inconsistent country data)

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Python** (Pandas, NumPy)  
- **PySpark** (distributed data processing)  
- **EDA & Data Visualization**  
- **RFM Analysis**  
- **K-Means Clustering** (customer segmentation)  

---

## 🧠 Analytical Framework

| Dimension | Key Question |
|----------|-------------|
| **Product** | What do customers buy? |
| **People** | Who are the customers? |
| **Place** | Where does revenue & churn come from? |
| **Growth** | How is the business evolving? |
